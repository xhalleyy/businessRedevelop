Cajun Fried Rice

Yelp Review from Lani J:

This was my first time at Tea Amo and I am hooked. The aesthetic is so warm and welcoming. Lots of seating, very bright and the staff were kind, I could tell they loved what they do! They answered all my questions about the different drink options, they have so many! We also ordered dinner from here and it was very good. The Cajun fried rice with shrimp was amazing so was the calamari and lemon pepper wings! If you are looking for something new and different TRY TEA AMO.
