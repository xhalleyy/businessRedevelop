about us

Tea Amo is a delightful tea room utilizing unique blend of traditional teas and Asian fusion cuisine. Our establishment captures the essence of a perfect tea experience!

Indulge in the wide variety of refreshing and flavorsome bubble teas available. From classic milk teas to fruity concoctions, our vibrant selection will surely satisfy your taste buds. Don't miss out on the flavorful Asian fusion dishes offered at Tea Amo. Whether you're craving savory snacks, hearty meals, or delicious desserts, the menu caters to all preferences!
